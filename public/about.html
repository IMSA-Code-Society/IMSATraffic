About IMSATraffic

IMSA Traffic is a student made website designed to be used by fellow IMSA students to help more efficently use space and find quiet places to study. We came up with this idea by seeing how universities try to control their traffic on campus and based our website off of their apps. This website counts internet connections in areas to calculate the number of people there so you have a better understanding of where people are in the school.
